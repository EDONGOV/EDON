# Exception Register

Track every intentional fail-open or policy exception here. Each entry should
have a named owner, a business reason, a review date, and an explicit expiry or
revalidation trigger.

## Review notes

- Exceptions should be rare and tenant-specific.
- Break-glass behavior must be documented and time-boxed.
- Revalidation should occur after every major release or material risk change.
